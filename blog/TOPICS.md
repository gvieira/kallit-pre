# Blog Topic Backlog

This document tracks potential blog post ideas organized by product phase. It is a living document intended to guide thinking and ensure a coherent narrative over time.

---

## Phase 0: Before the Product Exists
*(Framing, motivation, discomfort)*

Establishing honesty and setting expectations.

- [ ] **“Why I Wanted a Place to Think in Public”**
    *   **Intent:** Personal motivation, without mythology.
    *   **Category:** Reflection
    *   *Anchors the blog as a thinking space, not a launchpad.*
- [ ] **“The Problem I Kept Circling Around”**
    *   **Intent:** Describe the recurring idea without resolving it.
    *   **Category:** Reflection
    *   *Important: show uncertainty, not clarity.*
- [ ] **“Why I Almost Didn’t Build This”**
    *   **Intent:** Surface resistance, doubts, tradeoffs.
    *   **Category:** Reflection
    *   *This creates credibility early.*
